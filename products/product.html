The Zando — Where Craft Meets Nature

The Zando Planter Stand is a celebration of slow-made goods. Each piece is hand-shaped by artisans in Rajasthan, using sustainably harvested teak that only gets more beautiful with time. No two stands are exactly alike — slight grain variations and tonal differences are the hallmark of genuine handcraft.

Designed to cradle pots from 6" to 10" diameter, the Zando elevates your indoor or balcony garden with quiet elegance. Its tripod silhouette keeps it grounded while its turned legs add a sculptural quality rarely found at this price point.

Sustainably sourced, FSC-certified teak wood — durable for indoor & sheltered outdoor use
Hand-finished with natural beeswax oil — no synthetic lacquers or harmful solvents
Medium (18") stands at 46cm height — ideal for large floor pots and trailing plants
Load capacity up to 8kg — suitable for most terracotta and ceramic planters
Arrives fully assembled, gift-ready in FSC-certified packaging
